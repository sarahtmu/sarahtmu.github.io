Who Am I?

I graduated from the Internal Ph.D. Program in Cell Therapy and Regenerative Medicine at Taipei Medical University. My research interest is precision medicine in cancer treatment. I used bioinformatics, machine learning, and deep learning approaches for my study.

In the future academic journey, I would like to become a Bioinformatician, focus on cancer research. With strong background in medicine and well trained Artificial Intelligent (AI) approaches. I hope I can contribute to the development of AI in medicine and health care management.
